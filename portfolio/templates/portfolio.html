About Me

Nolusindiso Hleko is a multi-talented aspiring software engineer. Born in a tiny town called Jamestown in South Africa, Nolusindiso is currently a full-time student in Germany. She is now based in Busan, South Korea, which she has called home for the past five years. Although an engineering student first, she enjoys exploring her creative side through design. And with life spanned across three different countries on three continents, there is no doubt that she treats each day as an adventure.
This portfolio is a demonstration of responsive design. The main focus is to demonstrate her understanding of responsive web design via HTML and CSS by exhibiting her work throughout the spring 2022 semester..
