About this page

The design for this Travel Gallery started with a standard grid design. I love structure and order; therefore, the initial plan for the gallery was different. It was a traditional three-column design, but I decided to change it and challenge myself to do something different during the procedure. Not much thought went into the color scheme, though; similarities are coincidental.
This page is also fully responsive and only has one column on the mobile version.
