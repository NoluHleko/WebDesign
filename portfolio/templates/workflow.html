About this page

I did my sketches on paper due to pressure and time constraints. I also appreciate the ability to scribble something down as I design. The inspiration for this blog in both aesthetics and design was this Soledad WordPress Theme. I loved the cleanliness and simplicity of it. I have also fallen in love with CSS's grid layouts, a common theme throughout my designs. Some slight difference between the theme and my design is that I used more warm tones in the pictures. I also included a right sidebar, thus utilizing two grids instead of three for my blog posts. Although the social media icons were a pain resulting in a less than pleasing look in the mobile version, the homepage is fully responsive. The website can be viewed here
